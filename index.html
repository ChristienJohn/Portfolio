A little about me

I'm Christien, a student currently studying Computer Science at Simon Fraser University. I am always exploring the many fields of work that the industry has to offer. I wish to be a part of something that brings joy to people around the world, whether it be a video game or an app. Improving lives is my ambition

A hobby (and slight addiction) of mine includes playing video games of all genres. From smaller indie games like Darkest Dungeon, to bigger titles like League of Legends, I love to explore the different worlds that are create, as well as learning the creative mechanics that makes each game unique.

When I'm not at my PC grinding out games, I am often powerlifting at the gym as I find that working out helps keep both my body and brain in top shape.
